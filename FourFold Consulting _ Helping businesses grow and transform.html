Our Services

By focusing on profits and people, we help leaders understand how to deploy their biggest sources of capital.

We do this across four key areas.

 

Purpose & Strategy

We support organisations to discover, refine and realise their purpose and strategy. We help think through the financial, competitive, and societal impacts of choices with the aim of running more profitable and responsible businesses.

We will:

  • Uncover or discover purpose
  • Define and review strategic plans, vision and values
  • Guide how to govern, lead and live purpose

Value & Growth

We work with organisations to size, prioritise and realise opportunities so they can grow, transform and build strategic agility. We also assess organisational capabilities and help evolve them for successful execution.

We will:

  • Identify revenue and cost opportunities
  • Review acquisition and divestment options
  • Define and sequence transformation and capability agendas

Leadership & Culture

We work with CEOs and leaders to equip them and their teams to get ahead, get things done and get along. We help understand existing leadership routines and cultural habits and how these need to shift to achieve results.

We will:

  • Clarify the role, priorities and behaviours for an effective top team
  • Design and facilitate leadership programs and executive coaching
  • Identify areas of misalignment and outline a plan for change

Models & Systems

We help organisations explore the effectiveness of current operating models and structures to achieve goals. We define how best to organise work and set reporting and metrics to deliver results with impact and engagement.

We will:

  • Align organisational principles, structures and incentives with strategy
  • Improve business metrics, controls and management reporting
  • Translate strategic direction to technology and customer implications
